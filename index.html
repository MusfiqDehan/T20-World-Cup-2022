Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the centre of which is a 20-metre (10-yard) pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two batsmen and a bowler. The batting side scores runs by striking the ball with the bat, while the bowling side bowls the ball with the wicket. The game is played at the old-style international format, which is played on a rectangular field with a length of 110 metres (42 yards) and a width of 65 metres (27 yards).

Football

Football, also known as football or soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The ball is kicked with the foot to score a goal.

Hockey

Hockey is a team sport played on a ice rink, usually a ice rink with a central goal, in which two teams of six players each, opposing teams, score goals by shooting a puck through the goal.
